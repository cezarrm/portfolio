About me

Graduated in Visual Design from Universidade Positivo, I have over 7 years of experience as a Graphic Designer. Currently, I work at the Veeam Software, where I contribute as a brand designer, gathering stakeholder requirements and designing effective solutions.

I am increasingly focused on developing my skills as a Product Designer. I am dedicated, detail-oriented, and driven by curiosity and consistency, which I believe are essential for continuous learning and growth. I also have a strong interest in business strategy and the impact design can create across different areas of a company.

Process

Every project comes with its own context, and the process should be adapted to fit the specific needs of that project. There are established methodologies that can be applied and customized throughout the process — such as the Double Diamond framework for digital product design. Because of that, I consider myself highly adaptable and solution-oriented. I enjoy exploring new approaches, experimenting with different executions, and finding the most effective path for each challenge.
